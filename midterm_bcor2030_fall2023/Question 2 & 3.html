2 Dongkyu is about to transfer to our main campus but he is concerned with the flow of Dollar currency due to expensive tuition fees. He asks Taekyoung and JeWoong to analyze the flow of Dollar currency in Korean Won for the year 2023 over a period of 10 years. The flow of Dollar currency follows a normal distribution with a mean of 1,300 Won and a standard deviation of 100 Won. Assuming that the flow of Dollar currency is 1,400 Won:

  • What is the probability that the flow of Dollar currency in Won will fall below 1,400 won?
  • Illustrate the answer for a in chart using the Python code: "plt.fill_between"
